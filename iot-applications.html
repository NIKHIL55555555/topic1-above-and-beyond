SMART BUILDINGS

SMART BULDINGS is the bulding which allows to do some tasks like control building temperature, security, maintenance etc. using mobile devices and computers.It Includes the installation and use of integrated and advanced building automation system. Integrates technology and IoT solutions for issues such as overspend and inefficiency in building use and construction also Smart operations will help reduce wasted staff time and make your space smart. It should have the features like:

  • Real-time updates and centralized connectivity
  • High security and reliability
  • Low Cost Maintenance and lesser human efforts in decision making
  • Self sustainable and sufficient in terms of requirements
  • Minimal error in false case scenarios such as smoke sensor activating the fire alarm , motion sensor detecting motion and turning lights on etc.
